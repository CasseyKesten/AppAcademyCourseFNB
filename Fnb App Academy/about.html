All About Us in Every Tattoo

For me, tattoos are deeply personal, they are a reflection of everything i have lived, loved, and learned. Each piece of ink carries a story, a memory, or a piece of my soul that i wanted to keep close forever. Some mark moments of strength, others remind me of people who shaped who i am, and a few are simply symbols of who i am becoming. Together, they tell the full story of me, all about us, all about my journey, and everything in between.
